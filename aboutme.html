About the Developer

Welcome! My name is Jessie L. Duncan. I am a graduate of UCF with a B.S in Psychology and the Coding Boot Camp for web development. I have a strong passion for making a better tomorrow, because everyone wants to wake up and be happy. I want to help develop the solutions that make this world a better place.

Living in the Central Florida Area for 27 years it has given me a strong passion for the space program. However my main love will always be in psychology. I am currently working on my passion project named Be Brave. It is featured in my portfolio and you are more than welcome to take a glance at it. It is the way I have found to bridge my love for tech and psychology into one. I hope one day to integrate it into schools or even just the apple/android stores for commercial use. Mental health is something we should work with just as we work out and eat healthy for our physical bodies. I hope this helps bridge that gap.

Aside from my passion project and love for space I am actively looking for new opportunities in the Central Florida Area and beyond. I am a team player, hard worker, self starter, ambitious, quick learner and put my all into anything I do. If you would like to see my resume, click below.
